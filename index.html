Overview

The Endangered Species (ES) Program is a website under the administration of the U.S. Fish & Wildlife Service. It provides its users with a myriad of tools that help locate ES-related information. However, the website is outdated and disorganized. Through redesigning the website by improving its navigation components, we provided a smoother user experience.
